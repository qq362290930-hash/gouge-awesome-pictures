# 生图提示词模板

每张图片单独调用一次内置 `image_gen`。先把策略中的一个认知锚点填入模板，再根据具体文章删掉无关字段。不要把多张图片或多个观点合并到同一个提示词。

## 新图模板

```text
Generate one standalone 16:9 horizontal editorial illustration as a genuine raster image.
Article context: {用一两句话说明相关段落和受众}
Single cognitive anchor: {这张图只需要表达的一个核心意思}
Physical metaphor: {把抽象概念转成的真实物理关系和核心装置}
Composition and camera: {主体位置、前中后景、镜头高度、观察角度、留白方向}
Objects and materials: {2–4 个主要物件，以及纸、玻璃、木头、金属、亚克力等具体材质}
Action or spatial relationship: {物件如何连接、阻挡、分流、堆叠、平衡或变化}
AI狗哥 role: {物件足以表达时写 none；需要动作主体时，说明 AI狗哥执行的一个不可替代的核心动作。若使用 AI狗哥，在 prompt 中追加："A chibi black Shiba Inu dog character with thick black rectangular glasses, half-open confident eyes, two white forehead dots plus one tiny central dot, off-white chest fur, holding a matte black smartphone in right hand and making a peace sign with left hand. Cartoon vector art style with thick black outlines and flat cel-shading, rendered as a 3D material figurine within the photorealistic studio scene with real weight, contact shadows, and volume. Character height roughly 1/4 to 1/3 of frame."
Short Chinese labels: {1–5 个可选短词；不需要时写 none}
Color direction: {根据文章语义、场景材质和视觉平衡，自主选择 2–3 个协调强调色；说明主色、辅助色、可选第三色及各自使用位置}
Visual direction:
- Minimal, photorealistic 3D studio still life on a pure white or extremely pale warm-white seamless background.
- Soft large-source studio lighting, restrained fill light, coherent highlights, subtle natural contact shadows, and ample negative space.
- Real physical scale, thickness, weight, edges, surface texture, reflection and refraction. Paper must show thickness and fibers; glass and acrylic must show transparent volume and edge highlights; wood must show subtle grain; metal must show controlled material response.
- The main subject occupies roughly 35%–65% of the frame, leaving at least about 30% calm negative space.
- One image communicates one idea. Make the semantic relationship readable through objects and space before relying on labels.
- If Chinese labels are used, keep them short, sparse, legible, integrated naturally into labels or objects, and consistent with the scene perspective and lighting.
Do not create flat illustration, vector art, icons, clay render, low-poly art, toy-like plastic, poster layout, PPT infographic, formal flowchart, course slide, complex architecture diagram, UI dashboard, neon technology scene, busy background, decorative gradient, excessive text, large title, or unrelated props.
Exception: the AI狗哥 mascot character is allowed as the sole cartoon-vector figurine within the photorealistic scene, provided he maintains thick black outlines, flat cel-shading, and real 3D volume with contact shadows.
Do not simulate the result with SVG, HTML/CSS, Canvas, PIL, scripts, placeholder graphics, or a screenshot of 3D software. The output must be the actual bitmap generated by image_gen.
```

## 多图一致性

为同一文章生成多张图片时，在每个独立提示词中重复以下共同约束：

- 相同画幅、白色棚拍背景、光线柔和度和整体写实程度。
- 相近的镜头语言、留白密度与材质精细度。
- 保持相同的色彩气质、白平衡和明度范围；允许每张图根据主题调整主辅色及使用比例。
- 颜色必须克制并集中在局部物件、标签、连接件或视觉焦点，避免彩虹配色、霓虹色、平均铺色和大面积高饱和色块。
- 不重复核心物件和构图，除非文章语义确实要求系列递进。

并发生成时仍要使用独立提示词和独立调用。某张失败时只调整该张提示词。

## 编辑模板

```text
Edit the provided image and change only this issue: {用户指定问题或 QA 失败项}.
Preserve the original 16:9 composition, semantic meaning, object placement, photorealistic 3D studio style, pure white or pale warm-white background, materials, lighting direction, contact shadows, negative space, and image quality. Preserve all correct Chinese labels exactly. Do not add unrequested objects, characters, titles, labels, or decorative elements. Return a genuine raster image using image_gen.
```
