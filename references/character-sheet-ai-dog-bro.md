## Anatomy & Proportions

- **Body type**: Chibi/Q-version, roughly 2.5 heads tall, rounded soft body.
- **Head**: Large round head, approximately 1:1 width-to-height ratio.
- **Ears**: Upright triangular ears, always visible from all angles. Inner ear color: warm tan (#D4A373).
- **Eyes**: Small half-open confident eyes with a cool, knowing expression — one eye may appear slightly narrowed or winking. Always behind glasses. Expression is smug and self-assured, not sleepy.
- **Forehead markings**: Two small white oval dots above the eyes, symmetrically placed, plus one tiny central dot between them — a signature feature visible from front and partial side views.
- **Nose**: Small round black nose.
- **Mouth**: Subtle confident smirk, small upturned curve.
- **Paws**: Rounded three-toed paws with tan pads.
- **Tail**: Distinctly curled fluffy tail with white tip, forming a tight spiral curl visible from side and back views.
